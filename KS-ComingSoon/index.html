Who we are

We are a recruitment company providing placements and student’s internships in Europe. We work exclusively with bilingual candidates to connect them to a selection of language job opportunities in a wide range of industry sectors, across different locations in Europe.

At King Stage International we believe every organisation is different and so is each candidate; that is why we understand our clients' individual needs to provide tailored solutions and ensure satisfaction for both parties.

Our multicultural team is made of professional recruitment consultants who are specialised in identifying clients' needs and meeting them with the highest quality service.

Our aim

By connecting the most progressive companies with the most qualified career-minded individuals, King stage International is committed to offering an innovative and superior service that gives candidates and employers more quality over the recruiting process. As an intermediary between candidate and companies, our clients are:

• Organisations which need dynamic and qualified interns to work on short-term projects, or simply seeking fresh talents to join them for long-term contracts.

• Penultimate-year European students looking for work experiences.

• Recent graduates are eager to start their career and secure a job amid this competitive job market.

Each applicant's profile is rigorously screened by our experienced recruitment team to make sure they meet suitability and quality standards. We then provide companies with a selection of the most suitable profiles.
